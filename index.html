Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Ademii

I'm the founder of Esma Beauty Studio. I have been in the beauty industry for over a decade, building my experience across Asia, Europe, and the United States.

Two years ago, I decided to bring together everything I had learned and began working on creating my own studio. Esma Beauty Studio was built from the experience, passion, and dedication I've collected throughout my career.

Even with many years in the industry, I still prefer to call myself a professional student. The beauty industry is constantly evolving, and I believe it's important to continue learning, growing, and elevating my skills alongside it in order to provide the highest quality experience for every client.

Frequently Asked
Questions

A gel manicure typically lasts 2-3 weeks with proper care. Our premium gel products and application techniques ensure maximum longevity. We recommend avoiding harsh chemicals and using cuticle oil daily to extend the life of your manicure.

While we do our best to accommodate walk-ins when availability allows, we strongly recommend booking an appointment in advance to guarantee your preferred time slot and artist. You can easily book through our website or by calling us directly.

Yes! We offer nail repair services. Whether it's a cracked gel nail, a broken extension, or a natural nail that needs attention, our artists can fix it. Please call us to schedule a quick repair appointment.

Our Halal Manicure uses water-permeable nail products that allow water to pass through during ablution (wudu). We offer two options: Henna Manicure, which uses natural henna to nourish and strengthen nails, and Japanese Manicure, a traditional treatment using nutrient-rich products for a naturally glossy, healthy finish.

All of our artists are highly trained professionals. Our Top Nail Artists have additional years of experience and specialize in advanced techniques such as intricate nail art, complex nail extensions, and signature designs. Senior Nail Artists are skilled professionals who deliver excellent results for all standard and advanced services.

The 4-Hand Service means your manicure and pedicure are performed simultaneously by two technicians. It's an add-on service ($20) that saves you time while still delivering the same high-quality results. Perfect for clients with busy schedules!

We are open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend appointments, as they tend to fill up quickly.
