# 2\. About the Studio

Short Description:

Esma Beauty Studio is a modern studio dedicated to exceptional quality, refined luxury, and timeless elegance. Every service is performed with precision, care, and the highest professional standards to ensure beautiful, long-lasting results. Designed as a calm and sophisticated space, the studio offers an elevated experience where artistry, expertise, and attention to detail come together to enhance your natural confidence.

Meet the Owner:

Hi, I’m Ademii, the founder of Esma Beauty Studio. I have been in the beauty industry for over a decade, building my experience across Asia, Europe, and the United States.

Two years ago, I decided to bring together everything I had learned and began working on creating my own studio. Esma Beauty Studio was built from the experience, passion, and dedication I’ve collected throughout my career.

Even with many years in the industry, I still prefer to call myself a professional student. The beauty industry is constantly evolving, and I believe it’s important to continue learning, growing, and elevating my skills alongside it in order to provide the highest quality experience for every client.
